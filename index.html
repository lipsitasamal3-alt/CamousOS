💡 Resume Tips & Best Practices

✅

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong verbs like "Developed", "Implemented", "Optimised", "Led".

📊

Quantify Achievements

Add numbers to your achievements. "Improved performance by 40%" beats "improved performance".

🎯

Tailor to Job Description

Mirror keywords from the job description to beat ATS filters and appeal to recruiters.

📄

One Page Rule

Keep your resume to one page for fresher/internship applications. Two pages max for experienced.

🔤

Clean Formatting

Use consistent fonts, clean bullet points. Avoid tables, images, or fancy graphics for ATS.

🔗

Include GitHub/LinkedIn

Always link your GitHub with projects and LinkedIn profile. Recruiters actively check these.

🚀

Projects Matter Most

For freshers, 2-3 strong relevant projects often outweigh a high CGPA. Show, don't just tell.

✏️

Proofread Thoroughly

Typos and grammatical errors are instant red flags. Use Grammarly or have someone review it.
